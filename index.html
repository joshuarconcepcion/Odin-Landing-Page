This is NewJeans

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation. The group debuted in August 2022 and consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. NewJeans quickly gained popularity for their unique concept, which combines a retro aesthetic with modern music styles. Their debut single album "NewJeans" and its title track "Attention" received positive reviews and commercial success, establishing the group as a prominent name in the K-pop industry.
