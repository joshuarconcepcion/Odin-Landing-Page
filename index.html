This is Moon Honey

Moon Honey is a cozy coffee shop based in Taiwan that offers a unique and relaxing atmosphere for coffee lovers. It features a warm and inviting space with rustic decor and an extensive menu of artisanal coffee drinks made with the finest beans. Moon Honey is known for its signature honey-infused coffee creations and friendly baristas who are passionate about serving the perfect cup every time. Whether you're catching up with friends, working on your laptop, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, Moon Honey is the perfect place to unwind and savor the rich and aromatic flavors of freshly brewed coffee.

Founded in 2023, in Kaohsiung City

High quality beans, roasted locally
